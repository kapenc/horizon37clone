OUR SERVICES

360 Assessments

Our customised 360° assessments are designed to capture actionable anonymised feedback for our customers. Our bespoke approach enables meaningful analysis of externally perceived strengths, styles, and limiting weaknesses. We coach clients to choose actions and commitments to elevate performance.

LEARN MORE

Psychometrics

We provide in-depth evaluations and analysis of personality traits, behavioural styles, and natural aptitude and abilities. Our expert coaches will advise on a selection of suitable frameworks, guide you through the process, step-by-step, and help interpret findings and select priorities for action by the individual, the team, and the organisation as a whole.

LEARN MORE

Team Discovery and Alignment

Our specialised workshops transform teams struggling from varied challenges into high-functioning players with aligned goals, mutual trust, effective communication, strong collaboration, and sustainable performance improvement.

LEARN MORE

Leadership Coaching

Our immersive and transformative leadership coaching is tailored-made to meet each of our customer’s unique needs. We ask the tough questions with candour and respect. Together, we identify opportunities and challenges, and work to improve performance in specific and meaningful areas relevant to your vision and strategic priorities.  

LEARN MORE

Culture and Leadership Diagnosis 

We are specialists in understanding, diagnosing, and improving organisational culture. Our approach respects the uniqueness of each organisation and we work in partnership with our customers to unleash the leadership performance and organisational culture needed for business success.

LEARN MORE

Internal Coaching

Our expert-led internal coaching programmes create a coaching culture within our customer’s organisation. We train and supervise internal coaches, and together, we put in place a formal role and structure within the organisation. This programme is the catalyst for a positive coaching relationship among employees.

LEARN MORE

Developmental Workshops

Our team of subject matter experts deliver customised workshops aimed at addressing specific leadership challenges and building capabilities within the organisation. Our style is to maintain a ruthless focus on business goals and use a coaching approach, tailoring each workshop moment by moment to respond to real-life issues our customers are dealing with. 

LEARN MORE

Ninja Coaching Sessions

All the benefits of our regular leadership coaching sessions, condensed into a series of shorter sessions. The ninja programme includes a series of streamlined intensive sessions tailored for customers that are short on time.
